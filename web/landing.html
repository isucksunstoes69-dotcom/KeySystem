Why Keyforge

Everything you need
to license software.

Built for indie developers and small teams who sell software and want real control over their licenses — without handing everything to a third party.

Tamper-proof keys

Every validation is signed with Ed25519. A spoofed or redirected server can't forge a valid response — your public key verifies it client-side.

Hardware locking

Bind a license to one machine on first activation. Customer got a new PC? One click to release and re-bind — or let them self-serve once a week.

Expiry & revoke

Time-limited or perpetual, your call. Kill a leaked or refunded key instantly — the next check fails everywhere.

Reseller accounts

Give partners their own API key and panel. Multi-tenant by design — each reseller only ever sees their own customers and keys.

Admin dashboard

A clean control panel for keys, users, and bans — search, filter, create and revoke without touching a terminal.

Self-host, own your data

One JVM app + a SQLite file on a volume. Deploy to Railway, Fly or a VPS in minutes. Your keys, your database — no vendor lock-in.

How it works

Live in three steps.

From zero to issuing signed licenses in an afternoon.

1

Generate your keys

Run KeyGen once to create your Ed25519 keypair. The public key ships in your app; the private key stays on your server.

2

Deploy the server

Push the included Dockerfile to Railway, Fly or Render with a /data volume. Set your admin token and keys as secrets.

3

Issue & sell

Create licenses from the dashboard or API, hand them to buyers, and your app validates them on launch. Revoke or rebind anytime.
